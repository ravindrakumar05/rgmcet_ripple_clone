CODING

150

  • This is individual competition
  • No negative marks are provided
  • Students should have knowledge on C, C++ and JAVA
  • It's a three Level contest as follows:
  • Level-1 MCQ (Multiple Choice Questions)
  • Level-2 Problem Solving (Writing logic)
  • Level-3 System Test(Execution of given program)

PAPER PRESENTATION

150

  • Presenting a paper seems very difficult but in the end seems to be very simple and easy
  • Topics allowed for Presentation
  • Artificial Intelligence
  • Block Chain Technology
  • Cyber Security | Cloud Computing
  • Machine Lerning Algorithms
  • Other Related Topics

POSTER PRESENTATION

150

  • All posters must be original work, prepared by the applicant(s) and not previously presented at any other competition
  • Student registrant(s) may only submit or be a participant on one poster
  • Judging Criteria (to receive maximum points)
  • Coverage of Topic,Organization/Logical Sequencing of Ideas
  • Layout & Design, Mechanics, Presentation

WEB DESIGNING

150

  • Each team consist of two or at most three. Team should design the static template.
  • No internet facility will be provided.
  • For Each team systems wil be allocated.
  • No need to bring your own laptop.
  • Team upto 2-3 members
  • Each participant should pay individually

TECHNICAL QUIZ

100

  • Each team consists of 4 members
  • Teams will be formed by the organizer
  • It's a five round contest as follows:
  • Round 1 - Easy Round | Round 2 - IQ Test
  • Round 3 - Buzzer Round | Round 4 - Buzzer Round
  • Round 5 - Rapid Fire Round
